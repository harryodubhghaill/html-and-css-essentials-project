Wine Tastings

We want everyone to be able to enjoy and learn about wiine. Our tastings are a casual and open way to get into the wonderful world of wine!

What to expect at a tasting evening with us?

Wine shouldnt be fussy so we dont make a fuss! All of our tasting evenings are kept simple, with a basic theme, some nibbles to pair, and a bit of banter to keep spirits up. They will generally consist of:

  • 5 wines
  • Charcuterie board with seasonal ingredients
  • Sparkling water to cleanse the palate
